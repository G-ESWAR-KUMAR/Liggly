It's Fruit Magic.

Most treats are flavored water frozen into a stick. Liggly is different.

We take whole fruits, mash them into a rich, velvety pulp, and set them using natural plant-based methods. The result? A jelly-like texture that doesn't drip like a popsicle and bursts with fiber-rich goodness.

1

We pick ripe, premium fruits.

2

We pulp them (skins and all where possible).

3

We wobble-ize them into Liggly bars!
